EXECUTIVE ORDER

BY THE GOVERNOR:

appointed as a member of the Georgia Board of Examiners of
Licensed Practical Nurses, for a term of office ending April 1,
2015, to succeed the Honorable Jane Sipple, whose term expired on
April 1, 2012.
