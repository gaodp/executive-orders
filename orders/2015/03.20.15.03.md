EXECUTIVE ORDER

 

BY THE GOVERNOR:

ORDERED:

That the Honorable Walter J. “Joe” Westbury of Spalding County,

the Honorable Thelon A. Hamby III, whose term expired February
13, 2015.

The Honorable Walter J. “Joe” Westbury
212 East College Street
