EXECUTIVE ORDER

BY THE GOVERNOR:

IT Is HEREBY ORDERED: That effective January 8, 2015, the Honorable Thomas

Georgia in the rank of Brigadier General (Georgia) to

This 8th day of January, 2015.

GOVERNOR
